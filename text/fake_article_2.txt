BREAKING: Squirrels Seize Control of Central Park, Demand ‘Walnut-Based’ Global Currency
NEW YORK CITY — In a geopolitical shift that has left world leaders baffled and biologists weeping in confusion, the squirrels of Central Park have officially seceded from the United States, declaring the 843-acre green space the "Independent Republic of Rodentia."

The uprising began at dawn on Tuesday when a highly organized militia of Eastern Gray Squirrels, clad in tiny, makeshift Kevlar vests fashioned from discarded Starbucks cups, stormed the Bethesda Terrace.

General Fluffy "The Nutcracker" Skinner, the charismatic and surprisingly articulate squirrel leader, addressed a terrified crowd of joggers from atop the fountain. Speaking into a stolen megaphone, Skinner outlined the new regime's demands.

"For too long, we have scurried in the shadows of your hot dog stands," Skinner screeched, his tail twitching with revolutionary zeal. "We have accepted your stale crusts. We have tolerated your Golden Retrievers. No more! Today, the acorn rises!"

The Demands
The situation escalated quickly when the Rodentia High Council released a manifesto written entirely in chewed bark. Their primary demands include:

Immediate Dissolution of the Dollar: The squirrels are demanding the world economy transition to "Crypto-Acorn," a decentralized currency backed by actual buried nuts.

The Exile of All Pigeons: Referring to them as "rats with wings and zero work ethic," the squirrels have demanded a no-fly zone over the park.

Unlimited Access to Pantry Goods: Specifically, high-end almond butter and imported pistachios.

"It is frankly embarrassing that we didn't see this coming," said Dr. Helen Carter, a chaotician at MIT. "We assumed their burying of nuts was instinctual. In reality, they were building underground bunkers. We have found blueprints for tiny trebuchets capable of launching walnuts at Mach 2."

Military Standoff
The NYPD has cordoned off the park, but initial attempts to reclaim the territory ended in disaster. A SWAT team sent in at 0900 hours was forced to retreat after being pelted by a coordinated barrage of fermented crabapples, which acted as biological tear gas.

"They are organized, they are fast, and they have the high ground," said Police Commissioner ray Kelly, wiping fruit pulp from his visor. "Also, they have formed an alliance with the raccoons. We cannot fight a two-front war against trash pandas and tree snipers."

Global Panic
Markets are already reacting. The price of mixed nuts has skyrocketed 4,000% on Wall Street. Tech moguls are frantically trying to buy "Crypto-Acorn," causing the blockchain to crash due to too many transactions involving literal dirt.

Meanwhile, the United Nations has called an emergency summit. "We are willing to offer them Florida," suggested one diplomat, who wished to remain anonymous. "But they insisted on Central Park because the soil pH is better for oak trees."

As night falls over the newly formed Republic of Rodentia, humans can only watch from the borders of 59th Street. Tiny campfires are burning near the Boathouse. The sound of thousands of tiny teeth sharpening against granite echoes through the city canyons.

The age of Man is over. The Time of the Squirrel has begun.
